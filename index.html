The Modular Subsystems

A-TPU Kinetic Pump

Advanced Air Channels

Sub-millimeter tracks mapped directly onto calculated high-temperature zones of the human plantar surface for precise thermoregulation.

eVent Protection

Advanced open-pore material configurations allowing immediate vapor escape while maintaining structural integrity against sand, dirt, and water.

Silicone Cooling Pads

Interconnected anatomical comfort pads embedded with micro-channels that act as rapid conductors between the skin boundary and moving air streams.
